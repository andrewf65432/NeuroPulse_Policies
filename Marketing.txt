# NeuroPulse

**Tune Your Mind**

Frequency-verified binaural beats and isochronic tones for sleep, focus, meditation, and relaxation.

## What is NeuroPulse?

NeuroPulse is a brainwave entrainment audio app that uses scientifically-designed frequencies to help guide your mind into desired mental states. Unlike other apps, every NeuroPulse track is frequency-verified and crafted by audio engineers.

---

## Features

- **300+ Soundscapes** — Nature, ambient, music, pure tones, and therapeutic audio
- **Frequency-Verified** — Every track contains real, measured binaural frequencies
- **Two Beat Modes** — Binaural beats (headphones) or isochronic tones (speakers)
- **Adjustable Intensity** — Control frequency strength from subtle to immersive
- **Personalized Playlists** — Curated content based on your goals
- **Sleep Timer** — Drift off as audio gently fades
- **Offline Downloads** — Listen anywhere, no connection needed
- **Backdrop Mode** — Layer under Spotify, podcasts, or audiobooks
